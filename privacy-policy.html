Privacy Policy

TruckLedger Pro — by Mk Software

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TruckLedger Pro ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use TruckLedger Pro:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you register.
  • Financial Data: Load details, expense records, income, and deductions you enter into the app.
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns to improve the user experience.
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for compatibility purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the TruckLedger Pro service.
  • To sync your data securely across devices using Firebase.
  • To generate financial reports and summaries for your personal use.
  • To improve app features and fix bugs.
  • To communicate important updates about the service.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), which provides industry-standard encryption. Each user's data is isolated and protected — only you can access your own financial records.

We implement strict Firestore Security Rules that prevent unauthorized access to your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information or financial data with any third parties. Your data is yours and is never used for advertising purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

TruckLedger Pro uses the following trusted third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and database storage (Firebase Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play — App distribution

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and export your data at any time using the Export JSON feature.
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us.
  • Request a copy of your data.

7. Children's Privacy

TruckLedger Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
